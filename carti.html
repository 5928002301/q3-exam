Playboi Carti

Playboi Carti is an Atlanta-born rapper known for his unique baby-voice delivery, energetic ad-libs, and futuristic production. Rising to fame with hits like Magnolia and wokeuplikethis, he became a key figure in the SoundCloud rap movement. His albums, including Die Lit and Whole Lotta Red, showcase his experimental approach to trap music, blending punk aesthetics with hypnotic beats. With a cult-like fanbase and an ever-evolving sound, Carti continues to push the boundaries of hip-hop.
