Playboi Carti

Playboi Carti is a rapper known for his minimalist beats and catchy ad-libs. His projects, like Playboi Carti and Whole Lotta Red, have made him a key figure in modern trap music.

Lil Uzi Vert

Lil Uzi Vert is an American rapper known for his genre-blending style, mixing hip-hop with emo and punk influences. With hits like XO Tour Llif3, Uzi has become a trendsetter in both music and fashion.

Myron

Don Toliver

Don Toliver is a Houston-based artist who blends melodic trap and R&B. Gaining fame with his feature on ASTROWORLD, his album Heaven or Hell showcases his signature atmospheric sound.
