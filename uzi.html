Lil Uzi Vert, born Symere Woods, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for their energetic delivery, futuristic aesthetic, and genre-blending sound. Rising to fame with hits like Money Longer and XO Tour Llif3, Uzi became a leading figure in the emo-rap and SoundCloud rap movements. Their albums, including Luv Is Rage 2 and Eternal Atake, have topped charts, showcasing their signature mix of melodic flows and fast-paced beats. With a bold fashion sense and a cult-like fanbase, Lil Uzi Vert continues to push the boundaries of hip-hop and pop culture.
